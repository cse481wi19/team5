Demo Script

Start with person in chair, Ava is near dock.
Person says "GOOD MORNING", Ava says good morning and goes to start position.
Person says "Ava, I'M FEELING STRESSED," Ava responds and plays music.
Ava asks if Person is feeling better, respond with "YES PLEASE" or "YES THANKS".
Person asks "Ava, CAN I SEE YOUR BACKPACK", Ava moves towards them and shows backpack.
Person removes dog from backpack, tells Ava "THANK YOU" or "I'M DONE", Ava goes back to start position.
Tell Ava "GOOD NIGHT", Ava says good night and goes back to dock.
